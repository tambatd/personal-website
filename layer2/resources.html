Resources

Here's a non-exhaustive list of resources I've found useful over the years.

For iconography:

  • Signs, Symbols, and Ornaments
    • Great book on the first principles of iconography. Takes your through cave drawings to caligraphy.
  • Antique Pattern Library
    • Lists out patterns from different cultures. Great for inspiration.
  • Logo Book
    • Logos from different industries.

For UI Design:

  • Video Game Interface Wiki
    • Great place to look through UI designs in video games.
