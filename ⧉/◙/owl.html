Somewhere in the heavens...

Somewhere in the heavens there is an Owl.

For all of eternity, an endless labyrinth of books he presides over — never once growing tired.

There are Librarians adorning this labyrinth who scoff at his pensive nature: inspecting books at random, meticulously reading them.
