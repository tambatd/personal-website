People Who Inspire Me

Contrary to most designers—or people in general—most of the people I take inspiration from are my friends and/or other artists I've interacted with one on one. I am but the sum of my friendships and the people I've met over the years. Here are a few links to people who've changed my life for the better:

  • Alex Tomlinson 🐦
    • Alex is an incredible artist and friend. The font on this site—Nay Aug—was designed by him. You can usually find him drawing birds, designing typefaces, or working on pigeon post.
  • Kasra 📝
    • Kasra writes on Substack a lot. He has an incredible ability to speak to the heart in a way I've only seen in Anton Chekhov's writing.
  • Santi Grau 🇪🇸
    • Santi is...Santi? There's really no other way to describe him. I first met him while I worked at Tesla. He works on interactive art installations but saying just that would do a disservice to the culmination of his work. He is an artist in every sense of the word.
  • Alanna Tran 🎨
    • Alanna is an incredibly talented designer I met through playing minecraft with my friends Angelo and Raven. You should check out her comics. She has an ability to pack an incredible amount of emotion and detail into her artwork that I don't think I've ever seen anyone come close to.
  • Raven Mo 📮
    • I once had someone I just met mention Raven as "Famous Type Designer Raven Mo." Raven is one of the most talented designers I've ever met. She works on type design, iconography, UI design, and anything you could think on. Pure. Distilled. Creativity. If you like typography, check her work out.
  • Angelo 🇵🇷
    • Angelo constantly uplifts and tries to help everyone around him. When I say that, an idea in your head might pop up, but I can assure you it's a disservice to the amount he cares. Very rarely in life will you meet someone like Angelo, and that's very unfortunate. He's a software engineer at Railway, and a cracked one at that.
  • Chris Wang ™
    • Designed by Chris Wang ™. Chris Wang Design ™.
    Very rarely do you see people so versed in classical design tools. I genuinely think if there were a world where Figma and the entire Adobe suite didn't exist, you could give Chris a single 2b pencil without an eraser, and he'd be just fine. His photography is incredible, check it out.
  • Eva Zhang 🧮
    • If you're interested in the application of intelligence, AI as a tool to solve world problems, AI agents, crypto, or matcha, Eva is the person to talk to. She is unwaveringly herself, something most people aren't. More people should be like Eva.
  • Amir Bolous 👨‍💻
    • Amir started Rabbitholeathon, which is where both him and I first truly hung out 1 on 1. He's constantly building things, constantly learning, and I think what I appreciate the most about him is that he cares, a lot.
  • Yev 🤓
    • Yev is crazy, and I love him dearly. He's currently working on LSD (not the drug!!!) If you're interested in a company that can turn any URL into a SQL-compatible API check his work out.
