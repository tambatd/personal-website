Hey, I'm Devendra ❀

I'm a designer. For me that translates into working on iconography, building out design systems, making interactive experiences (video games, web apps, art exhibitions), or writing c++ code.

More importantly, being a designer translates into being someone who cares a lot. Someone who cares about the end to end experiences you have with everything around you, including this site.

Most of my design inspiration comes from the early works of Naoto Fukasawa and Yugo Nakamura. I prefer minimalism in the sense of being functional and not empty. Modern minimalism makes me want to vomit.
